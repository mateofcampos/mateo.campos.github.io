2022 Demo Reel

A demo reel featuring 2D animations of projects and creations I made for fun!

Procrastination (2022)

An original piece. As someone who suffers greatly from procrastination, barely getting schoolwork done during my elementary years, I wanted to make a silly story featuring my own original character.

Gas Prices in 2022 (2022)

An original piece. With the increase of gas prices this year, I wanted to poke fun and make an animation with my character who becomes flabbergasted with the recent skyrocket of gas prices, something that someone could relate to and have a laugh about.

Fish Bowl (2022)

Fan art. While learning how to get puppet animation done, I was tasked with making a fish move from one side of the canvas to the other, so I turned this into some Animal Crossing fanart, of my own villager and others watching a fish move in an aquarium, much like in the games.

Sitting Down (2022)

An original piece. Learning how to use ToonBoom Harmony, I was tasked with making myself sit down on a chair or sofa. A piece I liked since I worked with more in-frames and enjoyed my facial expression at the end of the video.

That's My Son | Smash Bros. Animation (2022)

Fan art. Using Harmony I recreated a scene using Smash Bros characters with audio from American Dad.
