Books4U

Ever get tired whenever you browse a website, getting overloaded with ads and losing focus on what you were even planning on doing? Does the issue become more problematic, when you're trying to look for or even purchase a specific book? With Books4U, we pitched a goal of creating a home page to your liking! Once you load the app or website, you'll be greeted to an assortment of your favorite books and genres, no more pesky pop ups or ads distracting you from trying to make a calm and collected browse through the website. In addition to adding your own creativity to the menu, you can toggle customizations to be recommended specific types of books and or authors.
